About Us

We have been in business for over 25 years. And we know what decorators and candy makers need because we are decorators and candy makers ourselves. In fact, our business started when avid baker Sepideh Pahlavani got frustrated that she could not find the supplies she needed to make perfect wedding cakes. Instead of crying into her cake, she opened the Confectionery House.
